Wife and Partner

I’m a strong believer in the power and importance of family — something I’ve admired since my childhood. Love has a beautiful way of crossing boundaries, and mine certainly did. I’m married to my amazing husband, Engineer Charles Kamau, who comes from Karatina in Central Province, while I’m from Nandi County.

“See how far I went for love?” 💖

How We Met

Our journey began in February 2019 at a friend’s party. Charles’ laughter, the spark in his eyes, and his lively, jovial personality immediately drew me in. Being an outgoing and social person myself, I knew I had found someone who could match my energy.

The Man I Married

Charles is kind, happy, and truly a person whose character is worth emulating. Over the years, he has brought so much joy into my life. We’ve shared unforgettable moments — like the time in Ol Pejeta, Nanyuki, when we were chased by a rhino and almost drowned… in our BMW! 😂 His love for meat often inspires him to cook for us on special occasions (not always, but I’ll take it!). “One thing I deeply cherish is his care and support, especially when I’m unwell.”

Our Marriage Journey

Marriage is not a bed of roses, but through the years, we’ve faced challenges hand-in-hand, always seeking solutions together. We’ve made it a point to nurture our bond through shared activities:

  • Celebrating birthdays and anniversaries with special outings.
  • Playing games like cards, pool, and darts.
  • Watching football together.

These little traditions have helped us grow stronger.

Lessons Learned

Marriage has taught me: Patience, Tolerance, Respect, and the value of truly seeing and appreciating each other.

“You cannot love others fully until you love yourself first.”

Looking Ahead

Together, Charles and I dream of building an empire, raising a happy family, and growing old side by side. “I believe in us — in our love, our friendship, and the beautiful life we are creating.” ❤ This is my love story… complete with laughter, lessons, and a lot of heart.
