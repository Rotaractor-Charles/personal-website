Faith Jebungei was born in the serene village of Kabongwa in Nandi County, the youngest of six children in the family of Mr. Lawrence Korir and Mrs. Constantine Korir. Her humble beginnings in the village shaped her values, work ethic, and determination to succeed, inspired by the remarkable achievements of successful people she admired growing up.

Faith began her education in 2002 at Fr. Kuhn Academy, Chepterit, before joining St. Joseph’s Girls, Chepterit, for her secondary education. She later pursued higher education at the University of Eldoret, where she earned a degree in Business Management, specializing in Accounting. With a deep passion for teaching and mentorship, she further advanced her qualifications by obtaining a Postgraduate Diploma in Technical Education from the Kenya School of TVET.

Beyond her professional journey, Faith is a lover of nature walks, traveling, and building meaningful friendships. She finds joy in connecting with people and experiencing diverse cultures and environments.

Faith is married to, a Software Egineer, Charles Kamau, and together they are blessed with two beautiful daughters, Aryan and Nyla, who inspire her every day to pursue excellence both in her career and personal life.

Her Beginnings

Born with curiosity and courage

Faith is always driven by a deep curiosity about the world around her. From a young age, she eagerly explored new ideas, embracing challenges with courage and determination.

This spirit of inquiry led her to pursue education and skills that would empower her to make a difference in her community. Her early experiences laid the foundation for a career rooted in learning, teaching, and leadership.

Her Passion

Driven by purpose, fueled by impact

What began as a spark of curiosity in the classroom grew into a calling. Faith saw education not just as a profession, but as a way to open doors, ignite potential, and help others see the possibilities within themselves.

Through each lesson, she strives to create a space where curiosity thrives and confidence grows—transforming knowledge into empowerment and learning into lifelong opportunity.

Her Vision

Empowering others to rise

Faith envisions a world where education unlocks every individual's potential, fostering confidence, curiosity, and resilience.

Her work focuses on creating inclusive learning environments that inspire growth, innovation, and a lifelong love of learning.
