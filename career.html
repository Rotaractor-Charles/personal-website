Career Journey

Faith is a dedicated accounting and business trainer with a passion for empowering learners through practical skills and innovative teaching methods. Her career journey reflects a steady progression from volunteer roles to key positions within technical and vocational education, contributing to student success and institutional development.

Volunteering Experience (June 2023 – August 2023)

Volunteer Trainer — Tetu Technical and Vocational College

During her volunteering period, Faith delivered foundational courses in accounting and business to diverse groups of students. Her key responsibilities included:

  • Teaching basic accounting principles such as bookkeeping, financial statements, and budgeting.
  • Introducing essential business concepts including entrepreneurship, marketing basics, and business communication skills.
  • Conducting practical workshops to develop students’ numeracy, problem-solving, and professional communication.
  • Assisting in curriculum review and adapting teaching materials to better suit student needs.
  • Providing one-on-one mentoring to students to improve their academic performance and career readiness.

Employment at TeTu Technical and Vocational College (September 2023 – April 2024)

Accounting and Business Trainer — Tetu Technical and Vocational College

As a full-time trainer, Faith was employed by the BOM, which expanded her responsibilities to include:

  • Designing and delivering comprehensive modules in financial accounting, management accounting, and business management.
  • Developing lesson plans focused on real-world applications such as small business management, financial analysis, and customer relations.
  • Facilitating entrepreneurship training, including business plan development, marketing strategies, and risk management.
  • Coordinating with industry partners to provide internships and practical exposure to students.
  • Leading assessment and evaluation activities, including continuous assessment tests and practical assignments.

State Department of TVET (August 28, 2024 – Present)

Accounting and Business Trainer — Tindiret Technical and Vocational College

In this role, Faith plays a pivotal part in the college’s academic and operational growth:

  • Teaching advanced accounting topics such as auditing principles, taxation, and computerized accounting systems.
  • Offering specialized courses on business communication, leadership, and customer service excellence.
  • Serving as the Income Generating Officer, responsible for identifying and managing projects that boost the college’s revenue streams, such as workshops, consultancy services, and training programs for local businesses.
  • Acting as a Policy Developer, contributing to the creation and revision of academic policies, quality assurance frameworks, and institutional guidelines to enhance teaching and learning standards.
  • Representing the college in the development and implementation of the Learning Management System (LMS), ensuring effective e-learning delivery and digital resource management.
  • Mentoring junior staff and supporting continuous professional development initiatives.

KNEC Exam Marker

National Examination Marker

  • Reviewing and marking national examinations in accounting and business studies.
  • Ensuring assessment consistency and upholding academic standards across all examined institutions.
  • Providing detailed feedback to help improve exam questions and assessment criteria.
  • Participating in training sessions and workshops organized by KNEC to stay updated on marking guidelines and educational policies.
